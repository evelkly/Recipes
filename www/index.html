Monkey Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 rolls of Pillsbury biscuits
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 stick of butter

Instructions

  1. Tear biscuits into quarters
  2. Mix sugars and cinnamon in a plastic bag
  3. Drop each quarter into bag and shake well to coat
  4. Place quarters in a dutch oven (use liners to reduce mess)
  5. Melt butter and pour over biscuits
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Serves 6 to 8.
